Giulio Marin

I was born in province of Treviso on September 14, 1989. I received the bachelor's degree ("Laurea triennale") in Information Engineering in 2011 and a master's degree ("Laurea magistrale") in Telecommunications Engineering in 2013 (110/110 cum laude) both from the University of Padova.
In the summer of 2013 I was intern at Imimtek (Palo Alto, CA) working on computer vision algorithms and demos for 3D human-computer interaction.
Since 2014 I'm a PhD student enrolled at the Graduate School of Information Engineering at the University of Padova.
My research interests are in computer vision, in particular in the acquisition and processing of 3D scenes acquired from multiple sensors.
