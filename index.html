Senior Computer Vision Engineer

Giulio Marin

I'm a Senior Computer Vision Engineer at Aquifi. Here I’m leading a team that focuses on delivering accurate data from the depth camera. I’m also working on anomaly and defect detection combining 3D geometry and image based deep learning techniques.
I got my PhD at the University of Padova, Italy, in 2016, with a thesis on combining color and depth information to improve the quality of the acquisitions for applications from gesture recognition to 3D reconstruction.
I have experience with camera calibration, color and depth processing, 3D reconstruction, and machine learning.
I have served and I'm serving as a reviewer and TPC member for ICIP, ICME and IEEE Signal Processing Letters.
