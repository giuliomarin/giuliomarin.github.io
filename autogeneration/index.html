Computer Vision Algorithms Engineer

Giulio Marin

I'm a Computer Vision Algorithms Engineer at Apple where I work on 3D reconstruction. Previously I was a Computer Vision Engineer at Aquifi, a startup that made 3D depth cameras and software for industrial applications.
I have experience with camera calibration, color and depth processing, 3D reconstruction, and machine learning. I deployed systems for machine vision ranging from portable devices to complex systems with multiple cameras, lighting and custom integration.
I got my PhD at the University of Padova, Italy, in 2016, with a thesis on combining color and depth information to improve the quality of the acquisitions for applications from gesture recognition to 3D reconstruction.
I have served and I'm serving as a reviewer and TPC member for ICIP, ICME and IEEE Signal Processing Letters.
